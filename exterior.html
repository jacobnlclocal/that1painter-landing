FAQs

Exterior painting questions

When can exteriors be painted in Knoxville?

March through early November typically. We don't paint below 50°F overnight, in active rain, or when rain is forecast within 24 hours of finish coat. We watch the forecast and adjust.

How long does a full exterior repaint take?

An average 2,000–3,000 sq ft Knoxville home takes 5–8 working days. Brick, three-story, or heavy trim houses can run 10–14 days.

Will the paint peel?

Not if it's prepped right. Peeling exterior paint is almost always a prep problem (skipped wash, no primer on bare wood, painting over mildew). Our process eliminates the usual causes - and we warranty it for 3 years.

Can you paint vinyl siding?

Yes - with the right primer and a vinyl-safe paint color. Dark colors on vinyl can cause warping, so we'll steer you toward colors rated for vinyl.

Do you repair rotted wood?

Minor wood repairs are usually included. Larger rot, replacement boards, or fascia work get quoted up front separately so there are no surprises.
