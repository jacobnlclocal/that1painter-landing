FAQs

Interior painting questions

How long does an interior repaint take?

Single rooms typically 1–2 days. Whole-home interior repaints in Knoxville take 3–7 working days depending on square footage, trim work, and color count.

Do I need to move out?

No. We phase the work room-by-room so you can keep living in the house. We can also schedule around work-from-home or school hours.

What paint brand do you use?

Sherwin-Williams Duration Home or Emerald, and Benjamin Moore Aura or Regal Select, depending on the surface. Premium paint is included in your quote — not an upsell.

Will the smell be bad?

We use low-VOC paints by default. Most rooms are safe to sleep in the same night, and the smell is gone within 24 hours.

Do you fix wall damage before painting?

Yes — nail holes, small cracks, and minor drywall damage are included. Larger drywall repairs are quoted up front so there are no surprise charges.
