The Story

Why this company exists

My parents got their house painted when I was a teenager. The painter took the deposit, painted about half the house, and then we never heard from him again. They had to chase him down to get the rest of the work done — and the work he did finish started peeling within a year.

A few years later they got it done again — by a big national franchise. This time the work was fine, but every conversation went through a 1-800 number, every change was a phone tag nightmare, and the price kept "clarifying" upward.

Both experiences had the same problem: nobody at the top cared what happened at your house. I built That 1 Painter of Knoxville to be the opposite of both. We're licensed, insured, organized like a real company — but you can still get me on the phone.

If you call and I don't answer, leave a message and I will call you back. Same day, business days. That's the deal.

How We Operate

Same checklist on every job

Owner-led estimating

I or one of my managers walks every job before we quote it. No drive-by guesses, no algorithms picking a price.

Fixed-price quotes

Itemized in writing. No "this might be more" language. If we miss something on the walkthrough, that's on us — not you.

W-2 employees, not subs

Our crew works for us. They show up because they want to keep their job, not because a sub-contracted day laborer needed cash.

Daily cleanup

You don't live in a job site. Brushes washed, tarps folded, trash hauled — every single day.

Final walk-through

We walk the project with you and a flashlight before payment. Anything flagged is fixed on the spot.

3-year warranty

Workmanship covered for 3 years in writing. If it fails, we come back. No fine print to argue about.
