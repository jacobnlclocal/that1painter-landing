The Story

Why this company exists

Most painting projects in Knoxville go one of two bad ways. Either you get a guy who takes the deposit and disappears halfway through - or you get a national franchise that puts you through a 1-800 number where every change is a phone-tag nightmare and the price keeps "clarifying" upward.

Both problems have the same cause: nobody at the top cares what happens at your house. That 1 Painter Knoxville is built the opposite way. Lucas and Nick - the people who give you the quote - are also the people who manage the crew on your job. Same names, same faces, start to finish.

If you call and we don't answer, leave a message. We'll call you back same day during business hours. That's the deal.

How We Operate

Same checklist on every job

Manager-led estimating

Lucas or Nick walks every job before we quote it. No drive-by guesses, no algorithm picking a number.

Fixed-price quotes

Itemized in writing. No "this might be more" language. If we miss something on the walkthrough, that's on us - not you.
