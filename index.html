FAQs

Knoxville painting questions, answered

What does interior painting cost in Knoxville?

Most Knoxville interior projects run $3–$7 per square foot of wall area, depending on prep, ceiling height, trim work, and paint grade. We give you the exact number in writing — no hourly billing — after a free on-site walkthrough.

Who's "we"? Who runs That 1 Painter Knoxville?

Lucas and Nick are the location managers for Knoxville. They run estimates, manage the crews, and walk every job with you. You get their direct numbers when you book.

How long does a typical exterior repaint take?

An average 2,000–3,000 sq ft Knoxville home takes 5–8 working days for a full exterior repaint, weather permitting. We coordinate around your schedule and the East TN forecast.

Do you do cabinet painting?

Yes — we spray cabinets with a factory-grade finish, either in our shop or on-site with full dust containment. Most kitchens are done in 5–7 days with hardware swapped at no extra cost.

What areas do you serve?

Knoxville, Farragut, West Knoxville, Maryville, Powell, Karns, Oak Ridge, Lenoir City, Alcoa, Sevierville, and most of East Tennessee within a 45-minute drive.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed in Tennessee, with general liability and workers' comp coverage. We'll provide certificates on request before any work starts.
